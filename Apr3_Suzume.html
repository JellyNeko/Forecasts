Suzume Movie Trip

Hello, everyone! A quick update regarding the Suzume film trip:
  • Location and Time: Sunday, 16 April at 2:30pm, in Vue at Shepherd's Bush (Westfield).
  • Please book your tickets quickly - we expect the cinema to get pretty packed! The link to do so is here: https://www.myvue.com/cinema/westfield/film/suzume-subtitled/times
  • Please save the receipt - it should be either in email form, or somewhere in your Vue account.
  • If you want to coordinate seating with your friends, feel free to use the #Suzume Movie Outing thread in our Discord.
  • One Claim per ICAS member. Non-ICAS members will not be able to claim their tickets, but of course you can still buy them and come watch it with us!
  • We will be subsidising a maximum of £8 off your ticket. You will have to complete the claim yourself - we apologise for this, but it is the approach that the union has suggested. Please contact committee if you need any help on this.
  • To be able to make a claim, you will need to complete the Claims quiz after logging in to eActivities: https://eactivities.union.ic.ac.uk/training/quizzes
  • Guide for the full Claim process: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1mL1E1w_n7lHDcCM5UpF_xwsd_nazqWXJfSvsPE5pHAc/edit?usp=sharing
  • Your claim should look like the image below. Copy-paste the text "Partial claim of £8 for movie outing event on 16/4/23 to subsidise the ticket purchase."
 

If you have any questions or need to know anything about the AGM, just email us at animesoc@imperial.ac.uk, or message us through Facebook or Discord!

Have a great break!

Ian, ICAS Secretary
