1. Monday: Anime Screening

6:30pm @ SAF G34
Theme: Bands

This week, to match our band's first ever performance of the year, we'll be watching three shows about bands:
  • Bocchi the Rock is about a shy girl named Bocchi who plays guitar. She ends up joining Kessoku Band, whose guitarist fled them before their first show. Finding happiness in performing, Bocchi and her bandmates put their hearts into improving as musicians while making the most of their fleeting high school days.
  • In Given, Mafuyu Satou owns a guitar but has not yet played it. After a chance encounter with a fellow student, who just so happens to be in a band, he drops in on a practice session and is inspired to learn the guitar!
  • Kids on the Slope is about three people, brought together by their mutual love of jazz: an introverted pianist (Kaoru), a delinquent (Sentarou), and the daughter of a record shop owner (Ritsuko).

3. Friday: Movie Screening

6:30pm @ SAF G34
Tokyo Godfathers

Here at ICAS we have a tradition. Our last movie showing of the Autumn Term is always Tokyo Godathers, the ultimate Christmas anime!

We follow three homeless people when they find an abandoned baby while rummaging through garbage on Christmas Eve. Appalled, Hana's maternal instincts kick in and she decides that they should look for its biological mother using the clues left with it: a key and a mysterious note. But they get caught up in a series of unprecedented events...
