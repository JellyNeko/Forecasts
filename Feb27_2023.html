1. Monday: Anime Screening
6:30pm @ SAF G34

Theme: The Anakin Special

This week, we'll be showing some of our Social Officer, Anakin's favourite anime!
  • In Saekano: How to Raise a Boring Girlfriend, Tomoya Aki wants to create his own visual novel, but the task is too big for him. He recruits childhood friend Eriri Spencer Sawamura to illustrate, literary elitist Utaha Kasumigaoka to write the script, and he directs. He choses to model the main character after classmate Megumi Katou. Will his game touch the hearts of those who play it?
  • Akebi's Sailor Uniform follows Komichi Akebi, who has always adored sailor uniforms. In fact, when she gets accepted into her school of choice, she can't wait to wear its uniform! But the middle school no longer uses sailor uniforms as its dress code. Komichi meets fascinating classmates as they experience school life together!
  • Ping Pong The Animation follows two high school friends, Peco and Smile, who are both talented table tennis players. Peco is outgoing and confident, while Smile is quiet and reserved. As they compete in local tournaments and face off against other skilled players, both Peco and Smile learn more about themselves and their relationships with each other.

3. Friday: Movie Screening
6:30pm @ SAF G34

This week we're watching Promare!

Thirty years ago, a new race of flame-wielding mutants suddenly appeared, destroying a large portion of humanity. These so-called “Burnish” have continued to appear at random, leaving a trail of death and destruction in their wake.

The autonomous republic of Promepolis is a thriving nation thanks to the incredible efforts of their leader, Kray Foresight, against the Burnish. A team of firefighters known as the Burning Rescue is tasked with stopping these horrifying monsters, using the most performant technology available thanks to their incredible mechanic Lucia Fex. Galo Thymos is an energetic young man, who considers Foresight his hero for saving his life and is the rescue team's most recent recruit.

A terrorist group calling themselves Mad Burnish has been causing havoc all over the nation. After an encounter with Mad Burnish leader Lio Fotia, Galo sets out on his fated journey to find the truth about these mutants, ultimately leading him to question everything he previously held to be true.

5. AGM Confirmed: 8 March 2023

For the first time in two years, our Annual General Meeting will be held in person! During the AGM, we will primarily be voting for Committee roles for next year. This means a few things!
  1. You must have membership if you wish to vote or run. Get membership here!
  2. On the day, if you are interested in leaving your mark on ICAS, simply nominate yourself! There will be time for a brief speech and then we will have an anonymous vote.
  3. If you would like to make other members aware of your intent to run, write up a manifesto and send it to our President on our Discord server - we have created a channel called #committee-manifestos on there where we will be displaying them.
    1. On your manifesto, please include your full college name and what position(s) you're running for.
    2. You do not need to have a manifesto for your name to appear in #committee-manifestos. If you just want people to know that you are running, send our President a message anyways - your name will be added to the channel.
  4. If you would like to ask the candidates any questions, we have created the channel #committee-questions for you to do just that. Remember to ping the candidate so that they don't miss your question!
  5. Empty positions after the AGM will be up for online voting during the Summer and Autumn.

If you have any questions about the process, feel free to either email us or message us on Discord. March 8 will be a Wednesday, and we don't have a room yet, but we'll be giving you more details closer to the day.
