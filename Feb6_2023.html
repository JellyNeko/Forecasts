1. Monday: Anime Screening

6:30pm @ SAF G34
Theme: Space Opera

This week, to fit with the theme of our movie, we will be showing some space opera anime!
  • Legend of the Galactic Heroes follows a new generation of leaders as they take power in two rival interstellar superpowers, the Galactic Empire and the Free Planets Alliance. Reinhard von Lohengramm climbs the ranks of the Empire, paralelling Yang Wenli of the Alliance. As ideologies clash amidst the war's many casualties, the two strategic masterminds must ask themselves what the real reason behind their battle is.
  • In Macross, humanity realizes it is not alone following the crash of an unknown spaceship onto Earth. The world unifies under the United Nations and repurposes the spaceship, naming it SDF-1 Macross. Unfortunately, on the day of its maiden voyage, a fleet of spaceships belonging to a race of aliens known as the Zentradi descend upon Earth, and the SDF-1 Macross, acting of its own accord, shoots down the incoming squadron, sparking an intergalactic war.
  • In Space Battleship Yamato 2199, humanity pours its remaining resources into the Yamato—an innovative, state-of-the-art battleship—in a last-ditch attempt to retrieve alien technology that will repair the Earth's irradiated surface after it was bombed by an expansionist galactic empire.

3. Friday: Movie Screening

6:30pm @ SAF G34
Galaxy Express 999

To cap off the week, we'll be watching Galaxy Express 999!

In the distant future, humanity has found a way to live forever by purchasing mechanical bodies, but this way to immortality is extraordinarily expensive. An impoverished boy, Tetsurou Hoshino, wants to purchase a pass on the Galaxy Express 999—a train that travels throughout the universe—because it is said that at the end of the line, those aboard can obtain a mechanical body for free. When Tetsurou's mother is gunned down by the villainous machine-man hybrid Count Mecha, however, all seems lost.

Tetsurou is then saved from certain death by the mysterious Maetel, a tall woman with blonde hair and a striking resemblance to his mother. She gives him a pass to the Galaxy Express under one condition: that they travel together. Thus, Tetsurou begins his journey across the universe to many unique planets and thrilling adventures, in hopes of being able to attain that which he most desires.

5. Doodle Event

It is time for our third doodle event! For those who haven't participated before, this is your time. All skill levels and art forms are welcome! The topics are:
  1. Rabbit
  2. Valentine's day
  3. Musical instrument
  4. Tattoo
  5. Alien

The deadline for this doodle event is 21 Feb 2023 (the doodle review will be the day after).

As usual, post your drawing(s) in #art on our Discord server and include '@Committee' doodle_event' in your message along with your title!

We welcome all skill levels to participate. Don't feel pressured to spend a lot of time on your drawing or to make it look perfect!

If you have any questions or need to know anything about us or our events, just email us at animesoc@imperial.ac.uk, or message us through Facebook or Discord! Do not reply to this email - we will not get your message.
