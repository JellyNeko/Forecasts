It was twilight on the Thames.

Yuni clutched ICAS’ hand in hers, hair billowing in the frigid winter wind. The freezing air seeped through her kimono and chilled her body to the bone, but her heart was warm.

"ICAS…”

They turned to each other. ICAS smiled. “This was a great date. We should -”

An ear-splitting crack shot through the silence and ICAS spun around, the fulfilled smile on her face suddenly turning to rage.

Yuni got a whiff of ozone as reality ripped itself apart like paper, a maelstrom of fire and multicolored gas surrounding the two of them. Behind it was chaos, a swirling mass of purples and reds and greens.

Out of it walked a pair of high-heeled boots, with leather made of infinite space and straps of the purest gold. The woman’s jaw, covered by a thick metal piece, creaked as she spoke, brown twintails flying wildly in the wind.

Before Yuni could react, she was looking down the barrel of a shotgun. The woman’s voice, harsh and terrible, pierced her ears.

“You’re coming with us,”she ordered.

Melon Band Summer Live

Melon Band is back again, with possibly the greatest lineup of songs we’ve had all year!
