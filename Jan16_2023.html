1. Monday: Anime Screening

6:30pm @ SAF G34
Theme: Sreekar's Mixed Bag

This week, I’ve picked out a few shows from a range of genres (mostly comedy though) that I think are a good watch:

Odd Taxi is about a walrus taxi driver who gets implicated in a ransom plot, and has to find his way out!

Eizouken is about a group of high school girls aiming to establish an animation studio in their high school, and the challenges they face with it.

Nanbaka, or The Numbers, is about a group of dangerous inmates trying various goofy antics to break out of the highest security prison in Japan.

3. Friday: Movie Screening

6:30pm @ SAF G34
Liz and the Blue Bird

This week we’re watching Liz and the Blue Bird, upon Ian’s request.

Liz and the Blue Bird is a story about two friends, the lively Nozomi and the quiet Mizore, who are chosen to play a duet section during a music piece. However, to play it properly, they will first need to understand the true nature of their friendship…

You don’t need to have watched the original show to see this movie - it’s a wholly separate story.
