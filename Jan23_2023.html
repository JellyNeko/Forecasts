1. Monday: Anime Screening

6:30pm @ SAF G34
Theme: Directors Showcase

This week, we hope you get to know some of the most prolific directors in anime! We've got 3 shows and 2 short films this week, each of which highlights a director with a unique voice or style. We start on Monday with three very distinct directors and series
  • Cowboy Bebop is directed by Shinichiro Watanabe, whose directorial style is famous for incorporating a variety of genres and styles - both in music and film. Cowboy Bebop follows Spike, Jet, Faye and Edward - four criminal hunters who make a living by collecting bounties on intergalactic outlaws.
  • The Tatami Galaxy is directed by Masaaki Yuasa, known for his distinct psychedelic, free-flowing, rough style and equally eclectic plots. The Tatami Galaxy follows a college student who feels bitterness towards all romance due to his own failures, and how he tries to change himself after traveling back in time to the beginning of his college life.
  • Kunihiko Ikuhara is known for stories with crazy concepts and surreal scenarios that often belie profound messages. Mawaru Penguindrum is about the brothers of a terminally ill girl, who are tasked by a spirit in a penguin hat with seeking out an item known as the 'Penguindrum' in exchange for saving their sister.

3. Friday: Movie Screening

6:30pm @ SAF G34
Angel's Egg / Garden of Words

This week we're doing a double showing! We'll be showcasing two works, one by Mamoru Oshii and the other by Makoto Shinkai.

Thanks to Ali, who suggested Angel's Egg this week. Directed by Mamoru Oshii, who is known for his thematically and visually complex films. The film follows a young girl who takes care of a large egg, and a mysterious man interested in said egg. They walk together through a desolate, alien landscape, questioning each other on a variety of existential topics.

And we end off the week with something slightly less heavy. You may know Makoto Shinkai for your name. and Weathering with You, but before he became famous he was known for contemplative, tragic romance films. Garden of Words is about a 15 year old student and 27 year old woman who meet regularly in Shinjuku Gyoen National Garden, and the ways in which they help each other grow.
