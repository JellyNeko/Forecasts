1. Monday: Anime Screening

6:30pm @ SAF G34
Theme: Industry

If you're currently hard at work looking for jobs and internships, come to SAF G34 on Monday to escape all that stress... by learning about an industry other than yours! This week, we'll be showing some anime that look at the process of making media: anime, manga, and movies!

  • We start off with Bakuman, an anime about two friends - a writer and an artist - who end up working together to write and draw their very own manga.
  • Shirobako follows five friends who, after making their own amateur anime, set out to become real industry professionals. Follow the trials and tribulations of producer Aoi, animator Ema, writer Midori, voice actor Shizuka and 3D animator Misa as they struggle to be recognised!
  • Seiyu's Life follows Futaba Ichinose, a rookie voice actor who is trying to catch her lucky break, taking on lots of roles as she struggles to improve her craft.

2. Wednesday: Social and Art Session

At 3:00pm, Hetty will be introducing us to volume construction - drawing 3D shapes from different angles!

Afterward, we will be screening the first six episodes of Tang Keke's Temptation, a webseries that takes footage from Love Live Superstar and, through the power of editing and voice acting, turns it into a soap opera of epic proportions!

Our very own members have been hard at work translating this series from its original Chinese, and we hope that you will come to see the fruits of their labour!

Remember to join our Discord server and check the announcements channel for updates on where the session will be held!


3. Friday: Movie Screening

6:30pm @ SAF G34
Pompo: The Cinéphile

To cap off the week, we'll be watching Pompo: The Cinéphile!

Joelle Davidovich Pomponette, better known as simply "Pompo," is an extremely talented movie producer, having inherited her grandfather's connections and cinematic eye. Despite her promising outlook, Pompo refuses to produce anything other than trashy B-movies. That is, until she hands her assistant, Gene Fini, a script for an ambitious screenplay about the life of a composer and announces that he has earned his first directorial credit.

Determined to make the film shine, Gene throws himself completely into his work. However, with an overwhelming workload and a deadline only days away, the aspiring director struggles with what must be sacrificed in the name of creation.

The deadline for this doodle event is 21 Feb 2023 (the doodle review will be the day after).
