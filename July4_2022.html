1. ICAS Artbook

Otto, Benson and Lucina are compiling an artbook that will be printed and distributed in October, so if you would like to submit a piece, , please read on!

The art must not contain any patented characters (aka OCs or ICAS-chan verse only)

 The art doesn’t have to be new, so feel free to submit older pieces!

Ideally make sure the art can fit on an A4 page, but this is more of a suggestion

Please submit your art by reaching out to Lucina, and try to submit by the 31st of July!
 

2. ICAS Artbook

As this is the final forecast, I’d like to take this opportunity to thank you all for reading this year! To be honest, I had a lot of doubts, whether my forecasts were engaging, or if I’m not keeping the standard up, but your kind words, and participation in the weekly questions has really cheered me up throughout the year.

As I was quite busy this year, I wasn’t able to attend most of the socials, but I hope that these forecasts made you feel like I was there in spirit! Ian will be taking over again from me, and I hope you’ll look forward to his forecasts! 

To those of you who just finished first year, I hope you can enjoy the summer break, and for those of you graduating, I wish you all the best in your future endeavours! Please come back and visit, because ICAS will welcome you with open arms!

Y’all did great this year, enjoy your break!
