1. Monday: Anime Screening
6:30pm @ SAF G34

Theme: TRIGGER

Studio TRIGGER was founded by anime legend Hiroyuki Imaishi, known for his over-the-top action and style. As the studio grew, however, its roster of directors has diversified, and so has its style!
  • Cyberpunk Edgerunners is set in a futuristic Californian metropolis. Teenager David Martinez attends a prestigious academy to fulfill his mother's lifelong dream to reach the top of Arasaka, the world's leading security corporation. But when he gets involved with a street gang and implants a device in his own back in a fit of rage, he is expelled - and this is when he meets a new source of income: the Edgerunners, cyborg criminals who will break any law for money.
  • At the beginning of SSSS.Gridman, Yuuta Hibiki wakes up in the room of his classmate and hears a mysterious voice calling his name from a nearby room. It comes from a robot named Gridman, who he must team up with to defeat giant monsters materializing within the city.
  • In Little Witch Academia, Akko has a single dream - to become a witch. Inspired by a magic performance she saw as a child, she wishes to be able to make others smile with her own magic. As she enrols in Luna Nova Magical Academy, in a time when wizardry is on the decline, Akko and her friends must learn the true meaning of being a witch.

3. Friday: Movie Screening - Double Feature:
Summer Ghost, Voices of a Distant Star 
6:30pm @ SAF G34

Based on suggestions by our wonderful members, we have our THIRD double feature of the year! This time, we will be watching Voices of a Distant Start and Summer Ghost.

In Voices of a Distant Star, close friends Terao and Nagamine are separated when Nagamine is selected for a military space expedition to retaliate against an alien invasion. They can only communicate through emails sent by cell phone, but as Nagamine journeys further, the time for each message to send increases.

Tomoya, Aoi and Ryou can see the Summer Ghost - believed to be the spirit of a young woman who committed suicide that appears in a specific area and can only be seen by those close to death.. It is said to be able to answer any question pertaining to death—something the three teenagers desperately want to know more about. But Tomoya isn't satisfied with their initial meeting, so he goes to find it once again.
