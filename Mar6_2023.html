1. Wednesday: AGM
2:00 PM, Room TBC
Vote for next year's Committee!

On Wednesday, we will be electing next year’s AnimeSoc committee at our Annual General Meeting (AGM). This is the first time we have done an in-person AGM in 3 years, so I must note that this year, you must be present if you wish to vote.

The location is yet to be decided - we applied for a room booking but the Union hasn't gotten back to us on that yet. We'll send out an email on Tuesday night / Wednesday morning to let you know where it will be. Be sure to come!

We'll be structuring the meeting as follows:
  1. An overview of our financial activities in the last year
  2. Committee Elections! We will be voting role by role, in the order of the list below. For each role, we will have:
  1. Introduction to the role and its responsibilities
  2. Speeches by interested candidates (up to 2 min each)
  3. Q&A from audience members
  4. Blind vote - we will ask everyone to put their heads down and vote by raising their hand

If you wish to run, you can do so by messaging our president Chun#2012 on Discord with your name, the position you are running for, and a manifesto (optional)! We'll distribute it to the rest of the society.

That being said, you can still run on the day even if you haven't expressed interest beforehand - just prepare a short speech.

Don't worry that you aren't experienced enough! Everyone on Committee works together to get things done, and we step in to help each other out from time to time.

There will be a handover, where your roles and responsibilities are explained in detail, sometime during the summer term. In addition, there is a handover document as well.

We look forward to seeing you!

There are 11 positions up for nomination:
  1. Chair - Leads and represents the society.
  2. Vice-Chair - Assists chair, manages room bookings.
  3. Treasurer - Approves claims and manages finance.
  4. Secretary - Writes forecasts and meeting minutes.
  5. Social Officer - Plans internal events, manages social media.
  6. Band Leader - Leads and manages the band.
  7. Vice Band Leader - Assists Band Leader in managing the band's activities.
  8. Technical Administrator - In charge of all things tech, from our website to Discord.
  9. Artist-in-Residence - Organizes art-related activities.
  10. Ambassador - Deals with inter-society stuff, and aids Chair with inter-uni events.
  11. Inventory Officer - Manages the library and keeps track of inventory.

3. Friday: Movie Screening
6:30pm @ SAF G34

It's been a few weeks since we last did a double feature. This week, we'll be doing two romance drama movies: The Light of a Firefly Forest and 5 Centimetres per Second! The total runtime of both films will be around 1hr 50 minutes.

The Light of a Firefly Forest follows Hotaru Takegawa. During a summer vacation at her grandfather’s house when she is six years old, our protagonist gets lost in a forest and is approached by Gin—a mysterious boy wearing a mask—who offers to help her. Overjoyed at the sight of another person, Hotaru runs to Gin with open arms only to be rudely fended off. However, she quickly learns the grave reason behind his behavior: a dreadful curse has been cast upon Gin. Should he ever be touched by a human being, he will disappear forever.

Though Gin urges her to never return, Hotaru does the exact opposite, and before too long, the two become close friends despite his delicate situation. Nonetheless, as years pass and their mutual feelings grow stronger, Hotaru and Gin start struggling with the boundaries that destiny has set between them.

5 Centimetres per Second is a romantic drama that focuses on the mundane and harsh reality of long-distance relationships. Takaki Toono and Akari Shinohara are childhood friends, but circumstances beyond their control tear them apart. They promise to stay in contact, and although the progression of time widens the distance between them, the chain of memories remains ever-present. Stuck in the past and unable to make any new memories, they cling to the hope of seeing each other again.
