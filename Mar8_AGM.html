AGM - 8 March 2023
 

Hello, everyone! Some updates regarding the AGM:
  • We will be holding the AGM in Room 407A, EE building, starting 2:00pm, today (March 8, 2023). This room is on the 4th floor of the EE building, the location of which will be indicated below.
  • We will be streaming the AGM in some capacity on our Discord channel if you can't make it physically, in #general_voice_chat.
  • Rather than voting through a show of hands, we will be using Microsoft Forms. You will need to log in to your Imperial account to vote - we will be checking for membership.
  • These forms will only be open for a short period of time.
  • We will be posting links in the #announcements channel on our Discord, as well as within the presentation itself.
  • We will not be holding an election for the Vice Band Leader role, as it has not yet been confirmed by the Union. This role will be available during our Summer by-elections.

The EE building is on the western side of Dalby Court.

To get there, you can either walk to the end of the Sherfield Walkway or take the stairs up from Dangoor Plaza (as shown below).

If you have any questions or need to know anything about the AGM, just email us at animesoc@imperial.ac.uk, or message us through Facebook or Discord!

Hope to see you at the AGM!

Ian, ICAS Secretary
