1. Monday: Anime Screening

6:30pm @ SAF G34
Theme: Romance

This week, we are watching three romance shows!

... what's that? We can't show that one?

Unfortunately, Sreekar was a coward and didn't want to show the greatest anime of all time, RENT A GIRLFRIEND. It truly is a shame. I have memes and everything. It could have been great. I could have screamed "IT'S RENTIN' TIME" and rented everyone. I could have given a five hour presentation on the god amongst men, Reiji Miyajima. But it's fine. But don't you worry, I'll get it on the list somehow.
  • In Ao Haru Ride, Futaba Yoshioka's childhood crush returns to school after a long absence - but he no longer is the warm, friendly boy she knew.
  • Tsuki ga Kirei is a cute coming-of-age romance story between two high schoolers - a writer and athlete - who are drawn to each other after a couple of chance encounters.
  • My Little Monster features two people, both of whom are withdrawn and apathetic people. And so after one confesses to the other, so begins a journey of self-discovery for both of them!
