1. Monday: Anime Screening

6:30pm @ SAF G34
Theme: Idols!

Idols! One of my favourite anime subgenres. It's always super uplifting to watch a group of girls work hard to achieve their dreams! This week, we'll be watching three idol shows:
  • Zombieland Saga is about zombie idols, as the title implies. Screamo included within!
  • Selection Project depicts a sort-of game show where nine girls must compete against each other to become the top idol.
  • Idoly Pride is about a group of aspiring young idols as they strive to live up to the legacy of an industry icon.

3. Friday: Movie Screening

6:30pm @ SAF G16
Perfect Blue

This week we’re watching Perfect Blue, which is definitely not a conventional idol anime.

Mima Kirigoe is an idol that recently retired from her group to pursue a career in acting, but as soon as she leaves, a series of terrifying events unfold. A blog detailing her every move pops up, gruesome murders occur, and a doppelganger chases her endlessly.

This movie contains some mild nudity.

5. Doodle Event

We are proud to announce the return of the Doodle Event! Started during the pandemic, it's become a mainstay of the society.

Each month, we give you five topics to pick from. All you need to do is draw something related to one of those topics and submit your drawing by the given deadline! There will then be a 'doodle review' in one of the Wednesday sessions where Hetty, our Artist in Residence, will be go through the submissions and give feedback. The topics for this month's doodle event are:
  • Halloween 👻
  • Fire 🔥
  • Betrayal 🔪
  • School 🏫
  • Ramen 🍜

To submit, post your drawing in #art on our Discord server and include '@Committee doodle_event' in your message (along with an optional title). Your doodle event submissions are due Friday 25 November. Good luck!

We welcome all skill levels to participate. Don't feel pressured to spend a lot of time on your drawing or to make it look perfect!
 

If you have any questions or need to know anything about us or our events, just email us at animesoc@imperial.ac.uk, or message us through Facebook or Discord!
