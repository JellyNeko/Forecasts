Fuck, I'm late, ICAS thought, twintails trailing behind her as she cycled furiously to campus. A piece of burnt toast clamped firmly in her mouth, pain shooting through her calves with each pedal stroke, she began berating herself.

First lecture of the year and you overslept. You were going to be better, weren't you? Why are you always like this? You set five alarms and still got up half an hour late. You're going to walk into that lecture theatre and everyone's going to stare, the lecturer's going to berate you for- OH GOD!

She'd been so deep in her own thoughts that she hadn't noticed the figure emerging from the interdimensional portal that had just opened in front of her. She screeched to a halt, heart pounding, as a familiar face emerged from within. Her own.

"Oh, how convenient!" the other ICAS noted, cybernetic arm whirring as she reached out to grab her.

6. Buy Membership!

Membership for the entire year is only £4, which can be bought here. With this tiny tribute, you get access to:
  • The ability to borrow from our library; full of manga, anime DVDs and drawing tablets for practice!
  • Come to larger events hosted by us, and the interuni events held jointly with UCL, LSE, and KCL.
  • Regular Monday, Wednesday, and Friday sessions do not require membership, so feel free to turn up to any of those throughout term.

All the money goes into funding our events for you guys, so the more we get, the more we can give back to you!
 

If you have any questions or need to know anything about us or our events, just email us at animesoc@imperial.ac.uk, or message us through Facebook or Discord!
