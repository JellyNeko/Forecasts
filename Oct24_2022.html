"Who are you?!" ICAS-chan yelled as the cybernetic arm reached forth. She tried to dodge, but with her legs on either side of a bike frame, it was rather difficult to do so.

The arm clanked into place around her wrist, squeezing her with a terrifying force.

"Stop! It- it hurts!" she yelled, tears welling up in her eyes. "Let go! HELP!"

"They can't hear you, ICAS-chan." the other version of her smirked. "I wouldn't be so stupid as to attempt this abduction in broad daylight. Right, we're leaving NOW!"

ICAS felt the wind leave her abdomen as she was pulled off her bike. Swirling energy enveloped the two of them, and in that split second ICAS knew, somehow, that she would not be returning to her world for quite some time.

1. Monday: Anime Screening

6:30pm @SAF G34
Theme: Magical Girls

Magical Girls! Girls, usually in high school, who secretly are powerful magic-users and who use their powers to defeat the forces of evil. Their shows usually come packaged with elaborate transformation sequences and cute animal mascots!

This week, we'll be watching:
  • Cardcaptor Sakura is magical girls, but with cards!
  • In Tokyo Mew Mew, magical girls are also cat girls.
  • Powerpuff Girls Z is technically a magical girl show?... They have transformation sequences, okay?!

3. Friday: Movie Screening

6:30pm @SAF G16
Kiki's Delivery Service

Kiki's Delivery Service is a feel-good classic by Hayao Miyazaki that follows the adventures of Kiki, a young witch-in-training who must must spend a year living in a distant town to become a fully-fledged witch.

With colorful characters and a beautiful setting, join us as we watch this classic coming-of-age story!
