"So... there are infinite versions of us?" ICAS-chan timidly walked through the dark corridors of Dark ICAS' lair. "And we all feel like you're missing something from our lives?"

"You don't feel it?" The mechanical arm whirred as it clenched into a fist. "Like something's been missing from your life? Like there's an endless hole in your soul?"

"No..." ICAS-chan shook her head. "I've never felt that."

"Well, you're one of the younger ones. Once you meet her, you'll understand." A pair of doors slid open to reveal a dark room, a small cage at the centre of it all. Within it was a girl, in a dress of blue and purple. A large pink bow keeping her hair tied back. And the moment ICAS laid eyes on her she felt it. A wave of overwhelming... fullness washed over her.

She doubled over. This was... this was everything. It was as if there'd been a gaping hole in her life that this girl would fill. And the knowledge of that was threatening to tear her apart.

As the edges of ICAS' vision began to darken, Dark ICAS walked over to her. "You see now? None of us can escape this reality. This hopelessness you feel? We feel it too. And we need to end it. Once and for all.

"Welcome, ICAS. Welcome to the Yuniverse."

5. Doodle Event!

We are proud to announce the return of the Doodle Event! Started during the pandemic, it's become a mainstay of the society.

Each month, we give you five topics to pick from. All you need to do is draw something related to one of those topics and submit your drawing by the given deadline! There will then be a 'doodle review' in one of the Wednesday sessions where Hetty, our Artist in Residence, will be go through the submissions and give feedback. The topics for this month's doodle event are:
  1. Halloween 👻
  2. Fire 🔥
  3. Betrayal 🔪
  4. School 🏫
  5. Ramen 🍜

To submit, post your drawing in #art on our Discord server and include '@Committee doodle_event' in your message (along with an optional title). Your doodle event submissions are due Friday 25 November. Good luck!

We welcome all skill levels to participate. Don't feel pressured to spend a lot of time on your drawing or to make it look perfect!
 

If you have any questions or need to know anything about us or our events, just email us at animesoc@imperial.ac.uk, or message us through Facebook or Discord!
