Welcome to the first AnimeSoc forecast of the new academic year! As Secretary, I want to take this opportunity to welcome you to ICAS.

We may be called AnimeSoc, but people here have lots of different interests. There's something here for everyone - even if you don't watch much anime! We have manga readers, vtuber fans, cosplayers, artists and much, much more.

We hope to see you at our Welcome Event this Friday, or on our Discord server, or at any of our social events throughout the year!

2. A Quick Introduction

Here at AnimeSoc, our weekly schedule consists of three sessions. You do not need to buy membership for these:
  • Monday, SAF G34 @6:30 pm: TV anime screenings. If you have any suggestions notify our committee!
  • Wednesday: Social sessions. This is usually our biggest weekly session. We're still trying to get the room booking for this, so do bear with us.
  • Friday, SAF G16 @6:30 pm: Movie screenings.

(These events will start on Monday the 17th of October.)

We will also have occasional music sessions on Saturdays. We will announce these on our weekly forecast. These are chill sessions - anyone is free to drop in and jam!

In addition, AnimeSoc is part of the London Anime Union, which was jointly founded by UCL, KCL, LSE and of course, us! The LAU hosts four inter-uni events each year, which you need to have bought membership to attend.

This year, we will be doing Karaoke, our annual Maid Cafe, a Halloween Party, and an event around New Years'. And the first event of this year is...
 

3. LAU Halloween Party

Our first event this year is our Halloween Party! We will be holding it the week before Halloween. Do remember to buy membership for this, and read our next forecast which should have more details. Come to dress up in your favorite cosplays, or just for the fun and games!

4. MCM Comic-Con

We're going to MCM Comic-Con this year! MCM Comic-Con is London's biggest annual convention for nerd culture, with stalls for comics, anime and video games.

If you're interested in coming with us, join the #MCM_Meetup thread on our Discord server so we can coordinate!
